I am Qiyang He (贺启旸), a second-year PhD student in the Department of Computer Science at Purdue University. I am supervised by Prof. Tiark Rompf. I am also a member of the Purdue University Programming Languages Group.

Previously, I received a B.Eng. degree in Department of Computer Science and Engineering at Southern University of Science and Technology by Prof. Bo Tang. I was an intern at SenseTime and ISG group at UCI.

My research interest mainly lies in programming language and database, especially in incremental view maintenance, multi-query optimization and generative programming. Currently, my goal is to use generative programming technique to optimize incremental view maintenance for multi-query.
