About

I just wrapped up my M.S. in Computer Engineering at UC Riverside, advised by Prof. Jiacheng Li. In 2026 I am joining the Database PaaS team at PDD / Temu, working on the storage layer that backs one of the world’s largest e-commerce platforms.

Before UCR I interned at TikTok (Trust & Safety) and iFLYTEK, and earned a B.Eng. from Southwest University under Prof. Bingyao Huang. I am drawn to distributed storage, query engines, and the engineering trade-offs that show up at e-commerce scale.

Outside work: weiqi (5 dan), lifting, film, craft beer.
